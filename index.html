About us

SwimSafe is a student-built organization developed by a small team from Case High School in Racine, WI Our mission is to promote a safer environment for all in our community. Our hometown is responsible for a wide variety of water-related injuries and deaths. With SwimSafe, we hope to inform others of the dangers of the beach and promote swimming safely.
